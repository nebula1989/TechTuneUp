Frequently Asked Questions

Need Help? Maybe someone has had a similar issue.

Check out these common questions.

My device won't turn on, or there is some other problem! I have either broken it or spilled liquid on it. What do I do?

  • Generally, physical or liquid damage related repairs are not covered under warranty. However, it is common to purchase additional protection plans such as "Apple Care", or "Accidental Damage Protection" or other type of insurance when you initially purchased the device. If you have purchased something like this, please continue with their repair process. If you don't know how to do this, I am happy to assist.

My device won't turn on, or there is some other problem, but I have NOT broken it or spilled liquid on it. What do I do?

  • The first thing to do is check and see if your device is still covered under manufacturer's warranty. If it is still under warranty, please continue with the repair process of your device's manufacturer.
